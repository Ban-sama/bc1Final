To Chocolate Heaven

Dark Chocolate

Less sweet than normal chocolate, a good source of zinc which boosts metabolism and immunity levels. Contains less dairy in the recipe.

White Chocolate

Has less caffine in the recipe than any other type of chocolate. Has the mildest flavor of all forms of chocolate. Contains cocoa butter but no non-fat cocoa solids.

Milk Chocolate

The smell of chocolate increases theta brain waves, which triggers relaxation. The caffeine content in a 1 oz bar of milk chocolate is 6 mg. That's 7 times less than a cup of coffe with minimal caffine.
